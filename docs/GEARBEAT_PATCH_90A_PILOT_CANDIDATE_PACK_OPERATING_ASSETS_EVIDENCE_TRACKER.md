## 2. Pilot Candidate Categories & Selection
Candidates are hand-selected based on their relevance to GearBeat's core verticals and their willingness to provide iterative feedback.

### 2.1 Candidate Categories
- **Studios**: Premium audio hubs in Riyadh/Dubai with verified gear lists.
- **Marketplace Vendors**: Pro audio sellers (new/used) with stable inventory.
- **Service Providers**: Verified engineers, producers, and voiceover artists.
- **Academy Instructors**: High-quality music/audio educators for live sessions.
- **Creators/Customers**: Verified community members for end-to-end journey testing.

### 2.2 Selection Criteria
- Technical proficiency with web tools.
- Existing social or professional reputation in the music industry.
- Commitment to a 4-week feedback window.
- Agreement to the **Invite-Only Pilot NDA**.
