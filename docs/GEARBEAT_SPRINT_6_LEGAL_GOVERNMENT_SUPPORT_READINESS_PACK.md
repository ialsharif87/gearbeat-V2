## 2. Support & Complaint Operations

### 2.1 Support Workflow
- **Tier 1: Self-Service**: Comprehensive Help Center with bilingual (AR/EN) articles covering vertical-specific inquiries (Marketplace, Studios, Academy).
- **Tier 2: AI-Assisted Discovery**: Presentational "Ask GearBeat" concierge for routing common requests.

### 2.2 Complaint Handling & SLAs
- **Acknowledgment SLA**: Target < 4 hours for high-priority complaints.
- **Resolution SLA**: Target < 48 hours for standard disputes.
- **Escalation Path**: Unresolved partner disputes are escalated directly to the Legal Operations manager.
