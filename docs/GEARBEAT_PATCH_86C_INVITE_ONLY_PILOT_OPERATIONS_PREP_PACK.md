# Patch 86C — Invite-Only Pilot Operations Prep Pack

## 1. Executive Summary
This document establishes the **Operating Model** for the future GearBeat Invite-Only Pilot. It defines the communication rules, feedback workflows, and safety boundaries required before the first pilot cohort is activated.

**Important Notice**: This document does **NOT** start a real pilot. All pilot activities remain in the preparation and planning phase.

---

## 2. Current Production Status
- **UI/UX**: Full Journey QA (84A-D) closed; premium dark/gold identity verified.
- **Legal/Safety**: Academy safety positioning (86A) and AI data governance (86B) established.
- **Technical**: Build-verified; PageSpeed optimized (98); Analytics foundations active.

---

## 3. Pilot Candidate Categories
Future pilot invitations will be extended to the following segments:
- **Studio Partners**: Selected Riyadh/Dubai hubs for discovery and intent testing.
- **Marketplace Vendors**: Pro audio sellers for inventory and storefront review.
- **Service Providers**: Verified engineers and voiceover artists.
- **Academy Instructors**: Early-adopter creators for live session positioning.
- **Selected Customers**: A small group of trusted creators for journey feedback.

---

## 4. Communication & Safety Boundaries

### 4.1 Invite-Only Rules
- **No Public Enrollment**: Pilot access is strictly by invitation via direct link sharing.
- **No Commercial Commitment**: Participation implies feedback contribution, not final commercial obligation.
- **Non-Transactional Status**: 
  - ✅ No live payments or Tap Gateway activation.
  - ✅ No real order fulfillment or booking confirmation.
  - ✅ No Academy paid enrollment or live video session delivery.

### 4.2 Data & Onboarding Restrictions
- **No Real Partner Onboarding**: No real-world contracts or PII processing yet.
- **No Real Document Processing**: Storage buckets remain restricted for internal test data only.
- **No AI Automation**: Support and discovery assistants remain documentation-only.

---

## 5. Operations & Feedback Workflow

### 5.1 Feedback Collection
- **Method**: Direct stakeholder interviews and lead capture form review.
- **Severity Tracking**: 
  - **S1 (Blocker)**: Critical UI or navigation failure.
  - **S2 (Major)**: Content inconsistency or mobile responsiveness issues.
  - **S3 (Minor)**: Typographical errors or aesthetic polish.

### 5.2 Evidence Capture
- **Requirements**: Screenshots, screen recordings, and session logs for any identified S1/S2 issues.
- **Storage**: Internal pilot evidence kits (81A) will be used for tracking.

### 5.3 Support Escalation
- All pilot-related queries will be triaged via the `/support` foundation and escalated to the core operations team.

---

## 6. Pilot Activation Decision Matrix

### 6.1 Go / No-Go Decision Criteria
- **GO**: All technical gates (Build/Lint/Typecheck) pass; Legal hub verified; Pilot-safe CTAs active.
- **CONDITIONAL GO**: Minor technical debt remains; non-critical content gaps identified.
- **NO-GO**: Payment or auth security leaks; broken core discovery journeys; regulatory non-compliance.

### 6.2 Exit Criteria (Before Real Pilot Execution)
- Completion of the **Pilot Launch Readiness Review (85A)**.
- Final approval of the **Legal & Safety Positioning (86A)**.
- Verification of **Internal Pilot Participant Roster (IPPR)**.
