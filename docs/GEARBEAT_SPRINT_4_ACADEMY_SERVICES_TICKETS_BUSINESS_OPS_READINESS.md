# Sprint 4 Business, Legal & Ops Readiness Pack — Academy, Services, and Tickets

## 1. Executive Summary
This document outlines the business, legal, and operational readiness for the **Academy, Services, and Tickets** verticals as part of Sprint 4. These verticals are currently in a "Demo-Ready" state, with UI/UX polished for luxury presentation, but are strictly prohibited from commercial execution until the Company Activation Gate (Patch 87A) is satisfied.

---

## 2. Vertical Readiness Status

### 2.1 Academy Readiness
- **Legal Positioning**: Established as a synchronous-only learning marketplace. No pre-recorded content.
- **Safety**: Minor safety policy (18+ or parental consent) implemented in policy documentation.
- **Regulatory**: Explicitly documented as non-accredited to manage government expectations.
- **Support**: Manual dispute resolution framework for live sessions established.

### 2.2 Services Readiness
- **Legal Positioning**: Professional Service Provider Agreement template ready for pilot partners.
- **Quality Control**: GearBeat-verified vetting standards for portfolio and professional credentials.
- **Support**: Lead-generation tracking and contact request logging active for demo.

### 2.3 Tickets Readiness
- **Legal Positioning**: Ticketing Partner Agreement template ready, covering revenue share and entry management.
- **Inventory**: Atomic seat/ticket reservation UI verified for demonstration.
- **Ops**: Entry management procedures (QR/Manual) documented for pilot phase.

## 3. Operational Infrastructure

### 3.1 Partner/Vendor Readiness
- **KYC Workflow**: Manual verification steps mapped for first pilot cohort (CR, ID, Bank verification).
- **Agreement Templates**: Standardized templates for Instructors (Academy), Vendors (Marketplace), and Studios.
- **Onboarding**: Mock-onboarding workflows finalized for QA testing.

### 3.2 Evidence Required for Live Activation
The following evidence is mandatory before any vertical moves from Demo to Pilot status:
1. **Commercial Record (CR)**: Reflecting relevant e-commerce and training activities.
2. **Business IBAN**: Proof of dedicated commercial bank account.
3. **VAT Registration**: Valid ZATCA/TIN certificate for tax compliance.
4. **Partner KYC**: Verified ID and business documents for all initial cohort members.

---

## 4. Prohibited Commercial Actions (Sprint 4)
To maintain the safety and legal integrity of the platform during development, the following are **STRICTLY BLOCKED**:
- ❌ **Real Partner Onboarding**: No binding contracts or live production accounts.
- ❌ **Real PII Collection**: No collection of official IDs, private bank details, or legal records.
- ❌ **Paid Academy Sessions**: No financial transactions for live lessons.
- ❌ **Live Ticket Sales**: No issuance of commercially binding tickets for real events.
- ❌ **Live Payments**: All checkout flows use the "Deferred/Manual" placeholder.
