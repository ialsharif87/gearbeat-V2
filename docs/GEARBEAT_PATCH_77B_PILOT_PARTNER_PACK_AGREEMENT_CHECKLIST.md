# GEARBEAT PATCH 77B: PILOT PARTNER PACK + AGREEMENT CHECKLIST

## 1. PURPOSE OF THE PILOT PARTNER PACK
The **Pilot Partner Pack** is a comprehensive set of documents and guidelines designed to ensure legal, operational, and financial alignment between GearBeat and its initial cohort of "invite-only" partners. This pack provides clear boundaries for the pilot phase, specifically addressing the manual nature of transactions and the deferred state of live commercial automation.

---

## 2. PARTNER AGREEMENT CHECKLISTS

### A. Studio Pilot Agreement Checklist
- [ ] **Scope of Use:** Definition of hourly booking slots available for the pilot.
- [ ] **Manual Payout Terms:** Agreement that payouts are processed manually within 5-7 business days of session completion.
- [ ] **No-Show Policy:** Manual verification of no-shows and resolution via GearBeat Support.
- [ ] **Facility Standards:** Confirmation that the studio meets "GearBeat Certified" baseline requirements.
- [ ] **Staff Access:** Designation of 1-2 staff members who will manage pilot bookings via the Admin UI.

### B. Vendor Pilot Agreement Checklist
- [ ] **Inventory Sync:** Commitment to update inventory availability manually or via CSV weekly.
- [ ] **Order Fulfillment:** Agreement to fulfill orders within 48 hours during the pilot.
- [ ] **Shipping Labels:** Use of GearBeat provided manual shipping labels/courier coordination.
- [ ] **Return Policy:** Acceptance of the manual return/refund pilot workflow.
- [ ] **Vendor Commission:** Confirmation of the pilot-exclusive commission rate (e.g., 0% or reduced for early adopters).

### C. Creator/Customer Pilot Consent Checklist
- [ ] **Pilot Participation Consent:** Explicit acknowledgment that the platform is in a "controlled pilot" state.
- [ ] **Payment Method:** Agreement to use manual payment/transfer methods as instructed (No Tap Live yet).
- [ ] **Feedback Requirement:** Agreement to provide 2 feedback surveys during the 4-week pilot.
- [ ] **Limited Liability:** Acknowledgment of potential downtime or manual resolution of errors.
- [ ] **Early Access Perks:** Confirmation of pilot-only rewards or credits received for participation.

### D. Service Provider / Instructor Pilot Checklist
- [ ] **Certification Verification:** Submission of valid professional credentials.
- [ ] **Insurance Coverage:** Proof of professional liability insurance (where applicable).
- [ ] **Time Slot Commitment:** Commitment to at least 2 sessions/classes during the pilot month.
- [ ] **Outcome Reporting:** Brief summary report of student/client satisfaction after each session.

## 4. PILOT OPERATIONAL RULES

### Manual Booking Pilot Rules
1. **Request Phase:** Customer submits a booking intent via the site.
2. **Review Phase:** GearBeat Admin reviews the slot availability manually with the Studio.
3. **Confirmation:** Admin confirms the booking and sends a manual payment link/instruction.
4. **Validation:** Studio checks the "Check-in" status on the pilot dashboard.

### Manual Marketplace Pilot Rules
1. **Purchase Intent:** User adds items to the cart and proceeds to "Manual Pilot Checkout".
2. **Payment Verification:** GearBeat Finance verifies the manual bank transfer or pilot payment.
3. **Dispatch:** Vendor is notified to ship the item via the coordinated courier.
4. **Delivery:** Delivery status is updated manually in the pilot order tracker.

## 5. DISCLAIMERS & SAFETY GATES

### No Live Payment / No Commercial Launch Disclaimer
> [!IMPORTANT]

> [!CAUTION]
