## 3. Pricing Approval Workflow
1. **Request:** Studio/Vendor submits pricing change via dashboard.
2. **Review:** GearBeat Finance reviews against market standards and premium quality rules.
3. **Approval:** Authorized change applied to the live storefront.
4. **Audit:** Quarterly review of pricing competitive positioning.

---

## 4. Manual Pilot Revenue Handling Rules
During the invite-only pilot (Phase 75), the following rules apply:
- **Zero Real Payouts:** No actual currency is moved through the Tap gateway.
- **Manual Reconciliation:** All "Paid" statuses are verified against manual bank transfers or sandbox IDs.
- **Audit Log:** Every pilot transaction must be documented in the **75A Evidence Register**.

## 6. Financial Handling Checklists

### A. Refund & Cancellation Checklist
- [ ] Verify refund eligibility based on the signed MSA.
- [ ] Initiate refund in the Tap portal (Sandbox).
- [ ] Adjust platform fee accounting to reflect the reversal.
- [ ] Notify partner and customer via support ticket.

### B. Partner Payout Readiness Checklist
- [ ] **IBAN Verification:** IBAN certificate on file and verified.
- [ ] **Tax Status:** VAT ID verified for the partner.
- [ ] **Minimum Balance:** Payout trigger amount (e.g., $100) reached.
