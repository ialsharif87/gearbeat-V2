# Sprint 5 Partner Onboarding & Admin Ops Readiness Checklist

## 1. Executive Summary
This document outlines the business and operational readiness for **Partner Onboarding** and **Admin Operations** for GearBeat V2 Sprint 5. While the technical UI and database schemas for onboarding exist in a demonstration state, this checklist defines the manual and governance-driven steps required to prepare for a future live pilot.

---

## 2. Onboarding Checklists by Partner Type

### 2.1 Studio Partners (Space & Gear Rental)
- [ ] **Profile Setup**: Studio name, luxury description, high-res gallery (min 5 photos).
- [ ] **Inventory Mapping**: List of gear included vs. gear available for additional rent.
- [ ] **Availability Rules**: Recurrence patterns, blocked dates, and minimum buffer times.
- [ ] **Evidence**: Commercial Record (CR) for Studio/Real Estate operations.

### 2.2 Marketplace Sellers (Equipment & Merch)
- [ ] **Seller Identity**: Business name, branding assets, and shipping origin address.
- [ ] **Product Catalog**: SKU definitions, variant mapping (color/size), and stock levels.
- [ ] **Fulfillment Strategy**: Confirmation of shipping provider and return policy adherence.
- [ ] **Evidence**: Commercial Record (CR) for Retail/E-commerce.

### 2.3 Creative Service Providers (Photography, Editing, etc.)
- [ ] **Portfolio Verification**: Link to professional portfolio or verified past work.
- [ ] **Service Tiers**: Clear definition of "Bronze/Silver/Gold" or "Hourly/Project" rates.
- [ ] **Response SLA**: Agreement to GearBeat response time standards (e.g., < 24 hours).
- [ ] **Evidence**: Freelance License or Business CR.

### 2.4 Academy Instructors (Live Lessons)
- [ ] **Curriculum Review**: Submission of lesson plans or course objectives.
- [ ] **Technical Check**: Verified high-speed internet and audio/video equipment for live sessions.
- [ ] **Safety Compliance**: Agreement to "Minor Safety" protocols and live-only synchronous learning rules.
- [ ] **Evidence**: Professional credentials or relevant certifications.

### 2.5 Ticket Organizers (Events & Workshops)
- [ ] **Event Definition**: Date, venue (GearBeat Certified studio or external), and capacity.
- [ ] **Ticket Tiers**: Definition of Early Bird, General Admission, and VIP perks.
- [ ] **Entry Management**: Assignment of staff for QR scanning or manual guest list check-in.
- [ ] **Evidence**: Event Management license or proof of venue partnership.

---

## 3. Admin Review & Operations Process

### 3.1 Stage 1: Document Verification (KYC)
- [ ] Review Commercial Record (CR) validity and expiration.
- [ ] Verify Tax Identification Number (TIN/VAT) with relevant regional authority.
- [ ] Confirm Business IBAN matches the legal entity name on the CR.

### 3.2 Stage 2: Quality & Aesthetic Audit
- [ ] Review all partner-submitted images for "Luxury Dark/Gold" brand alignment.
- [ ] Audit listing descriptions for tone, clarity, and bilingual (AR/EN) accuracy.
- [ ] (Studios Only) Verify "GearBeat Certified" status via physical or video inspection.

### 3.3 Stage 3: Technical Sandbox Activation
- [ ] Provision of "Partner Dashboard" access in a non-production/simulated environment.
- [ ] Test-booking or test-order placement to verify vertical-specific logic.

---

## 4. Required Evidence (Master List)
Partners must provide digital copies of the following to move to Stage 1 Review:
1. **Commercial Record (CR)**: Reflecting relevant activities.
2. **National ID / Iqama**: For the authorized signatory or business owner.
3. **VAT Certificate**: (If applicable) ZATCA/relevant authority document.
4. **Bank Account Proof**: Official letter or voided check with Business IBAN.

---

## 5. Support & Issue Workflow
- **Level 1 (General)**: Onboarding assistance via chat/email (e.g., "How do I upload my CR?").
- **Level 2 (Compliance)**: Document rejection disputes or tax verification issues.
- **Level 3 (Technical)**: Integration bugs or dashboard access failures.
