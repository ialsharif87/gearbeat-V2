## 3. Product Verticals
The platform operates across five core integrated verticals:
- **Marketplace**: Curated high-end creative equipment, gear, and merchandise.
- **Book Studios**: Real-time availability, atomic booking, and scheduling for professional creative spaces.
- **Services**: A professional directory for creative services (photography, cinematography, editing, etc.).
- **Tickets**: Event ticketing and access management for creative workshops and community gatherings.
- **Academy**: A structured learning environment for skill development, certification, and masterclasses.

The following are intentionally deferred and blocked until formal company registration and payment activation:
- **Live Payments**: Payment gateways (e.g., Stripe/Hyperwallet) are mapped but not activated. Transactions currently use "Deferred/Manual" mode for testing.
- **Live AI Execution**: The "Ask GearBeat" AI concierge is UI-ready but awaits LLM API key activation and vector search deployment.
- **Commercial Launch**: Public-facing marketing and commercial studio onboarding are pending official government trade licensing.
- **Automated Settlements**: Payout automation is pending legal verification of vendor bank accounts.
