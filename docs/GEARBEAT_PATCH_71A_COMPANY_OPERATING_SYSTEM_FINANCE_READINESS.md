## 4. Revenue Model Readiness

GearBeat's revenue streams must be tracked with granular precision within the finance ledger.

### A. Revenue Streams
1.  **Studio Bookings:** Commission-based (e.g., 10–20% per session).
2.  **Marketplace Sales:** Transaction fee (e.g., 5–10% per item) + shipping margins.
3.  **Ticketing Hub:** Service fee per ticket (fixed or percentage).
4.  **Premium Listings:** (Future) Subscription or "Boost" revenue for studios/vendors.

### B. Revenue Tracking Readiness
- [ ] **Ledger Mapping:** Each transaction must map to a specific stream.
- [ ] **Manual Reconciliation Gate:** Weekly audit between platform records and bank deposits.
- [ ] **Refund Reserve:** Maintenance of a "float" for manual refund processing during the pilot.

## 5. Monthly Reporting Template

Monthly "Pulse" reports will be shared with stakeholders:
1.  **Revenue:** Total Bookings vs. Marketplace vs. Tickets.
2.  **Growth:** New Studios, New Vendors, New Creators.
3.  **Retention:** Repeat booking rate.
4.  **Ops:** Support tickets resolved vs. pending.
5.  **Burn:** Total monthly operational expenses.
